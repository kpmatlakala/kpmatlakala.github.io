Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation Time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tbsp of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat butter in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  3. Pour in the eggs and let cook without stirring.
  4. Once edges set, gently lift with a spatula and tilt the pan.
  5. Add fillings on one half, then fold and slide onto a plate.
